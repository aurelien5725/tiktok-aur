Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Who We Are

This application ("the App") is a personal, non-commercial tool built and operated by Aurélien, based in France. It is not a product, service, or company.

2. What Data Is Accessed

The App accesses the following data from TikTok's API, exclusively for the authenticated account (the App's owner):

  • Basic profile information (username, display name, follower count)
  • Video list and per-video metrics: view count, like count, comment count, share count, publish date, title

No data from any other TikTok user is accessed or collected.

3. How Data Is Used

The data is used solely to analyze the performance of the owner's own TikTok content and inform personal music marketing decisions. It is not used for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose.

4. Data Storage

Metrics data is stored locally on the App owner's personal computer. It is not uploaded to any cloud service, database, or third party.

5. Data Sharing

No data is shared with any third party. Ever. This App has no backend server, no analytics platform, and no external data recipients.

6. Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any analytics tools.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)
